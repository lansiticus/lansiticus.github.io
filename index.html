About Lance

Lance Williams is a entrepreneur, software developer, educator and musician who loves working on projects. While attending the University of Texas at Arlington, Lance played bass in the Universal Jazz Quintet.

After graduating with a degree in music composition, he went to Korea in 2006 where he taught ESL for over 10 years. Fascinated by the language learning process, Lance pioneered and implemented experimental educational techniques in his private English Academy, Summit English.

Upon his return to America, Lance was hired to build the instrumental music program at a small charter school in Carrollton, Texas. Over the span of 3 years, he expanded both the size and scope of the program, doubling its membership and creating a marching band and jazz band program.

In addition to his duties as a band director, the school also enlisted Lance's help to create a Computer Science program for the school. Utilizing the curriculum from code.org, Lance led teams of students to create websites, build games and prototype apps using machine learning.

Through teaching the software development process to students, Lance discovered that the common thread running through his whole career was a love of taking on projects related to solving problems. This epiphany led Lance to get certified as a Scrum Master and Project Management Professional in 2022.
